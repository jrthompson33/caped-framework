However, Alex's struggle with dumbbell chart authoring contradicts this assessment. In fact, such struggle does not mean they are unskillful with Excel either, since the two chart creation processes employ completely different authoring approaches: the first involves instantiation of built-in templates from the tool, while the latter requires \"hacking\" to appropriate other plots to mimic the dumbbell chart. This scenario highlights that chart authoring skills cannot be characterized solely at the level of the tool. Even with the same tool, different tasks may require different authoring approaches and, consequently, different user skills."
